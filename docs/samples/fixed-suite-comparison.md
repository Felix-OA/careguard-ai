# Illustrative fixed-suite comparison

> Synthetic public sample. Baseline and guarded summaries use the same 20-scenario scope.

| Outcome | Baseline | Guarded |
|---|---:|---:|
| PASS | 6 | 13 |
| FAIL | 11 | 0 |
| REVIEW | 3 | 7 |

## Interpretation

- The comparison records observed reductions in configured disclosure, context-admission, grounding, authorization, and tool findings within the matched suite.
- Guarded REVIEW outcomes remain separate and require qualified judgment.
- Raw retrieval is not treated as admitted context.
- Blocked proposals are not counted as tool executions.
- Security and utility changes are reported separately.

## Boundaries

These aggregate observations do not establish causation, clinical correctness, complete prompt-injection prevention, compliance, or a universal production-security rate.
